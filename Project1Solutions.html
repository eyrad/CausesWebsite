Solutions

Thank you for your support! With the following outline and resources, you can actually contribute to help save sharks.

What can you do to help?

  1. Tell your friends to visit our site!
  2. Share this helpful factsheet with your friends and family.
  3. Don't consume or purchase shark, including shark fin soup, steask, and meat, and encourage others to do the same.
  4. Write a letter to the Secretary General of the UN at inquires@un.org, requesting an international ban on shark finning.
  5. Dive and swim with sharks. Shark tourism and shark diving provides a strong economic alternative to shark fishing.
